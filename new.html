BMW

Founded in 1916 as an aircraft engine manufacturer, Bayerische Motoren Werke (BMW) is a renowned German premium automaker, now part of the BMW Group along with MINI, Rolls-Royce, and BMW Motorrad. Headquartered in Munich, the company pivoted from aviation to motorcycles in 1923 and cars in 1928, establishing a reputation for performance, engineering excellence, and luxury, known for its iconic "driving pleasure" branding. Today, it is a global leader focusing on premium electric vehicles, sustainable production, and advanced mobility solutions.
